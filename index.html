Anton von Sturzenbock

Bio

Anton von Sturzenbock is a Western Australian artist specializing in portraiture and human figure paintings and drawings.

Anton works in a wide variety of media, most notably:

  • Pencil
  • Charcoal and chalk
  • Oil
  • Watercolours
  • Digital media

Sources of Inspiration

Although not a landscape artist, Anton appreciates beautiful Australian nature and wildlife. His favourite destination is Bremer Bay, WA, a community of 200 people some 500 km south-east of Perth where he can escape the hustle and bustle of city life. He also highly regards the works of some Western Australian portraiture artists such as Drewfus Gates and Tessa McOnie.

Music Projects

Anton is also very fond of music. Now and again, he takes out his guitar or keyboard and produces some wonderful tunes.
